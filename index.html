12 years of experience

Engage with a distinguished embedded software company offering over 12 years of extensive experience across a wide range of products and industries. In-house hardware lab

Our experts will bring up and debug your PCBs within our in-house hardware laboratory, equipped with a comprehensive range of professional test instruments, including oscilloscopes, testers, laboratory power supplies, soldering stations, and microscopes. The facility also features single-board computers, popular development kits and evaluation boards, as well as an FDM 3D printer for creating mechanical prototype enclosures.

Skilled engineers

With a dedicated team of experienced embedded software engineers, we deliver customized embedded software engineering services tailored to the unique requirements of your projects. We are equipped to undertake challenges and tasks of varying complexity, providing efficient and effective engineering solutions.

High security & quality management standards

Ensure the highest standards of embedded security and quality throughout the development lifecycle,By partnering with us, you benefit from a secure development process spanning project planning to production, safeguarding your software intellectual property and data confidentiality.

360° service

Flexible cooperation models
